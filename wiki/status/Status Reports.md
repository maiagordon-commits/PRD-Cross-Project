# Status Reports
> This page organizes weekly, monthly, and ad hoc status reporting for Maia's projects.

## Summary
Status reports should summarize progress, risks, blockers, decisions needed, milestones, and stakeholder updates. Maia's preference is to keep mainly summaries, action items, and issue analysis in the vault while live task status remains in Jira or the relevant tracker.

## Key Facts
- Useful report inputs: Jira status, Slack updates, meeting notes, risk register, dependency register, and project pages.
- Reports should be concise, practical, and source-backed.
- Use the status template for recurring updates.
